Free agent guard Gary Payton II has reached an agreement with the Portland Trail Blazers on a three-year, $28

million deal that contains a player option and extra incentives, league sources told Yahoo Sports. Payton II,

part of the Golden State Warriors’ 2022 NBA championship team, had a career season in the Bay in 2021-22. He

averaged 7.1 points, 3.5 rebounds and 1.4 steals over 17.6 minutes per game. He suffered a broken elbow in the

2022 postseason, but returned during the NBA Finals. This deal cements Payton II as a solid NBA contributor
